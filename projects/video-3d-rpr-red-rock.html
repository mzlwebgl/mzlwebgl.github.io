3D RPR Interactive Narrative – Red Rock

A role-play-reading (RPR) interactive narrative experience combining multi-character storytelling, 3D exploration, and puzzle-driven reasoning.

This project is a 3D role-play-reading (RPR) interactive literary experience inspired by the classic narrative Red Rock. Users progress through the story by exploring 3D environments, interacting with narrative-driven characters, and solving reasoning-based puzzles supported by clue cards and progression tracking.

The system includes multi-role scripted dialogue, clue-based hint mechanics, item collection, and virtual character voice narration to guide users through the narrative flow. All interactions are designed to support structured reasoning rather than linear reading.

The application was developed using Unity and deployed on Android devices. It has been publicly exhibited in cultural and educational environments, including library-based interactive installations.
